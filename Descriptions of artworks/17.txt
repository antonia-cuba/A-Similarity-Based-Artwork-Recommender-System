This piece of art depicts an orange cat in profile. It has orange fur with some cream undertones. The animal appears to be sleepy because its eyes are closed. Additionally, it has a golden ring—possibly a halo — circling its head. Long whiskers cover the cat's muzzle.
The color in the back is black.
